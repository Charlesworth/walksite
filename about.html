About Us

Hello, my name is Kady (yes like mean girls, no my parents did not name me after mean girls). My partner, Charlie, and I have decided to quit our jobs, sell our worldly possessions and attempt to thru-hike the Pacific Crest Trail. For those of you who haven’t spent the past few months ferreting out and frantically bookmarking all that the internet has to offer on this topic, the Pacific Crest Trail is a continuous maintained path through the USA, which stretches 2659-ish miles from the Mexican border to the Canadian border, winding it’s way through California, Oregon and Washington.We are intending to walk the entire length of it in one trip. This is known among hiker’s / outdoorsy types as a “Thru-hike” (I understand that the jargon only gets worse from here on out).

Check out our home page for a map (drawn by Charlie using Paint) showing the rough route of the trail up the East coast. As we add posts to our blog you should be able to see how far we have walked and where we are posting from.

I am a newbie hiker and Charlie is a little more experienced, however there is no doubt that this will be a huge challenge for the both of us! Charlie has built this site (from scratch!) as a way for me to document our adventures and reassure family and friends that we remain alive and just as crazy as ever.

We currently live in Hampshire England, though both of us are from the Southwest (for any international readers that is the pointy toe of the shoe area at the bottom). I grew up in gorgeous Devon and Charlie in slightly inferior (in my biased opinion) Somerset. I am a commercial property solicitor and Charlie is a software developer. It is slightly depressing that this small paragraph can sum us up so succinctly, but I am sure that that will change!

We hope you enjoy sharing our journey, if you do please visit again and if you are really keen - sign up to our mailing list!
