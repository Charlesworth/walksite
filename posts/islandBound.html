Endless Leg Day

Island Bound
9.494274, 99.935232

18/06/2016


We are currently on a bus on our way to Kho Samui, we left our hotel early this morning and got on the bus at 9am. In total it will take us 6.5 hours to reach the island via bus and ferry. I have been spending my time alternating between reading and staring out the window. I have been in Thailand for almost a month now but it is no less strange to me than when we landed.

We have now left Phuket Town with its erratic traffic and mixture of new and dilapidated buildings for lush green countryside. I don't know whether Thailand is always this beautiful or whether it is a perk of the rainy season but as I look out the window there is a sea of jungle green flowing past with tree and vine covered mountains like gigantic slumbering beasts in the background. The road we are on is pretty intact and we keep driving past little clusters of houses or possibly shops or both, it's hard to tell. The buildings are small and basic and almost ramshackle with clothes usually drying outside but I have noticed that most corrugated iron rooves have a satellite dish bolted to them, reminding me that despite the humidity and the different food things are not so different from home.

We are excited to go to the islands after spending 3 weeks at Tiger Muay Thai, for me the weeks passed pretty quickly, though the Charlie and I both struggled more with motivation during the last week. We finished our last day with 3 classes of Kickboxing, Muay Boran and Boxing and the went to a local stadium to watch the Friday night fights. We had a brilliant evening though we highly suspect that most of the fights were fixed... We leave the camp with quite a few bruises and scrapes from sparring but I am so glad that we went because it reminded me how much I love training in martial arts.

We have two of Charlie's friends, Elliott and Dodge flying out to meet us on the 21st, we have planned to spend the 8 days with them island hopping and taking an open water diving course in Kho Tao. I have never been diving before and am really excited to try it!
