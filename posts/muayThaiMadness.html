We flew down to Phuket on May 27th with plans to spend 3 weeks here at martial arts training camp, Tiger Muay Thai. We have just completed the first week and it was great! Its so wonderful to get back to martial arts, between moving to Southampton and not finding a club that clicked for me and work last year I haven't trained properly in months. We are taking a mixture of classes including kickboxing, muay Thai, yoga, Western boxing and BJJ. Getting back to it is hard - my arms aren't what they used to be, but really rewarding. The gym is on a street in the Chalong region which is made of entirely of boxing / fitness gyms, healthy restaurants, protein bars / cafes (who knew there were such things), massage parlours and accommodation. Pretty much everyone you see is in phenomenal shape or working towards it. We hope we will fit in by the end of the three weeks! Tiger is the biggest gym on the street and certainly the most commercial and busy. This works for us as people who aren't in tip top shape and who haven't trained for a while as we don't want to get are arse's handed to us by the locals! There is a broad range of classes and lots of other English speaking attendees to make friends with. We are each attending between 4 - 5 hours of classes a day.
