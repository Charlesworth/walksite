Endless Leg Day

Itchy Feet 50°57'57.5"N 1°22'06.9"W

16/03/2016


It’s lunchtime on my last Wednesday in the office before I jack it all in to hike the PCT. I am finding it impossible to get my head around the fact that this time next week I will be unemployed, despite that fact that I gave my notice 3 months ago.

I am 27 and though I have travelled a fair amount I have never been “Travelling”. Lots of my friends took some time before or after university to fly off and explore, but I (ever sensible) went from school to university and straight into a job. I don’t regret getting my head down and ploughing ahead to qualification, however I have alway had a small voice in the back of my mind bleating about a desire to see the world. This voice has become more and more insistent recently and I have had the feeling that a pinky sized version of me has been on the verge of of kicking a hole through my skull and stowing away on the first plane out of London.

Light Heart Gear - So-Long 6

In order to prevent sluggishness and possible brain damage we decided that 2016 was to be our year. Neither of us are advanced enough in our chosen careers to mean that we can’t leave without jeopardising our futures, we’ve had well enough paying jobs for the past year which have enabled us to save up and both of us have been suffering from increasingly itchy feet that no amount of weekend trips or annual holidays will satisfy. I am torn between being sad to say goodbye to my friends and colleagues and unbelievably excited / anxious / terrified / ready to jump on the plane.

There is so much to organise, my to-do list is slowly getting smaller but I am still daunted by the amount of stuff I have to get rid of! Somehow we will get through everything on the list done and fly out on April 7th to LA. After a night in a cheap airport motel we will catch the Amtrak down to San Diego where we will spend a day or so getting supplies and probably attempt to paint the town red on Friday night before getting serious with a sunrise start at Campo on Sunday 10th.

P.S Charlie has already quit his job and has just messaged me to say that he has set our new tent (the fabulous So-Long 6 from Lighthearted Gear) up in our back garden.
