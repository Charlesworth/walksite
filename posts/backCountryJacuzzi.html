Endless Leg Day

Back Country Jacuzzi
34.345565,-117.229934

29/04/2016


The morning was awesome, the afternoon was dreadful. We had a leisurely start, leaving the campsite at 8 (!) we hot footed it 2 miles up the trail to the hot springs. We had heard a brilliant account of the springs from a hiker names Oblivious at around mile 100 and we had really been looking forward to it. The springs did not disappoint, there were various pools which had been lined with concrete to form natural hot tubs. We spent about 2.5 hours lounging about in the hot water and plunging into the freezing Creek.

Soon we were back to the trail and back to reality, the climb up from the Creek was unforgiving as was the following few miles of contouring a hot and dusty mountain. We saw our first nudist hiker who passed us on the mountain heading to the springs, she certainly looked like she was having a great time. We struggled on through the rest of the afternoon ending up at mile 320. My emotions swing so quickly on the trail.
