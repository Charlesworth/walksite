Endless Leg Day

Listing is good for the soul 50°57'57.5"N 1°22'06.9"W

25/03/2016


I love to list. Its almost an addiction, a habit which I picked up from my Mum. When I had a job I spent the day working through my to-do list, deriving disproportionate pleasure from crossing off items and making it to the bottom of the page. At the moment my to-do list for the PCT is a bit intimidating, so I have decided to distract myself with a few, less daunting, lists :)

Things I am going to miss:

  1. Tea.
  2. My PC (primarily playing Age of Empires 2).
  3. Roast dinners.
  4. Bubble baths.
  5. My hot water bottle.

Things I am not going to miss:

  1. Bills.
  2. Traffic.
  3. The constant hum of the two motorways that encircle the small town where I live.
  4. The phone ringing.
  5. Washing up and cleaning the house.

What I am looking forward to:

  1. The scenery.
  2. The Challenge.
  3. Spending lots of quality, uninterrupted, time with Charlie.
  4. Getting back my fitness (and then some!).
  5. The people we are going to meet (though, truth be told, the idea of American hospitality both intrigues and scares me a little. I can't help it - I'm British).

I am a bit of a worrier, in fact sometimes Charlie literally has to sit me down and tell me to stop. If I am not careful my brain just runs away with me. It would be pretty easy for me to list 20+ things, but I will try and cut it down to 10. What I am nervous about:

  1. Water (finding it).
  2. Food (carrying enough).
  3. Creepy Crawlies.
  4. Being weak.
  5. Not enjoying the trail.
  6. Pooing in a hole.
  7. Getting lost.
  8. Bears / Mountain Lions (I'm from the UK, we have no scary wild animals. While writing I googled "UK scary animals" and found this article which made me laugh).
  9. Running out of money.
  10. Charlie or I getting injuries.

Writing them out has been pretty cathartic. Most of them are surmountable and the ones that aren't will either happen or they wont.
Anywhoo, above is the content of my brain for this evening; thank you for reading.
